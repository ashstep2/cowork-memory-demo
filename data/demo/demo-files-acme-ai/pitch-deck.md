# Acme AI - Pitch Deck

## Company Overview
Acme AI is building the operating system for enterprise automation. We use AI agents to automate complex business workflows that have traditionally required human judgment.

## Problem
Enterprises spend $4.5T annually on knowledge work that could be automated. Current RPA tools are brittle and require constant maintenance. LLMs enable a new paradigm: agents that can handle exceptions, learn from feedback, and adapt to changing processes.

## Solution
Our platform allows non-technical users to deploy AI agents across their enterprise workflows:
- **Agent Builder**: No-code interface to define agent behaviors and guardrails
- **Workflow Engine**: Orchestrate multi-step processes with human-in-the-loop controls
- **Integration Hub**: Pre-built connectors to 200+ enterprise systems (Salesforce, SAP, Workday, etc.)
- **Observability**: Real-time monitoring, audit trails, and explainable AI decisions
